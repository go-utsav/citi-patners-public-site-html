Our People

Meet our expert team delivering exceptional financial services in London

Ibrahim Ian Thompson

DipFA Certs CII (MP + ER)

Principal

25+ Years of Excellence

Ibrahim Thompson has been in financial services for over 25 years advising both individual clients and SME's on their financial planning needs. He has established an international presence particularly advising ultra-high net worth clients across the Gulf on matters ranging from high value property investments to meeting investor visa requirements.

Islamic Finance Pioneer

Having a special interest in Islamic Finance, he was one of its earliest pioneers on the advisory scene being critically involved in the launch of the first UK authorised shariah compliant collective investment fund in 1999.

Ibrahim retains his pioneering spirit and is currently working on the development of further innovations in the field.

Did you know? Ibrahim also holds a black belt in Taekwondo!

Your trusted financial consultancy offering expert mortgage solutions, business funding, and financial planning services.
